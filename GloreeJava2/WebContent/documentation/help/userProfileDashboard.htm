Title: User Account Profile Dashboard

 

Synopsis:  A single dashboard page where the user can go to modify his account profile, account type and billing information.

 

Use Case:  A user may want to change his email address or password . He may want to change his billing information. He may want to change his account type. i.e from ReadOnly to ReadWrite. From Read Write to Cancelled. This page should let them do that.

For Future : Consider how large organizations like Cisco can manage their users and their account status in bulk. For example, they may want a site admin (say an Ops Team) to manage the billing and account access on a single dashboard or automate it with an internal Grouper Tool.

How it works:

 

Step1: When you sign in to TraceCloud.com, using your account, you are taken  to you User Dashboard’s ‘Project Dashboard’ tab. Right next to it is the ‘Account Profile’ tab. Click that.

 

Step 2:  You can use this page to  manage your Account Details

·         Modify your email Id on file

·         Modify your first / last names.

·         Change your password

·         Reset your favorite pet’s name. Please remember that if you lose your password and would like to use reset it for you, we will ask you for your pet’s name.

 

Step 3: You can use this page to manage your Account Type.

An account in the Trace Cloud system could be either a) Trial , b) Read Only , c) Read Write ,d) Expired.

·         When you sign up to try the tool for the first time, you typically get a Trial account. The Trial account gives your FULL READ WRITE permissions on the system for 60 days.

·         Once the 60 days in the trial period expire , your account status is set to ‘Expired’. At this point, your information is preserved in the system, and if you can convert to a regular user mode to access your information.

·         There are 2 type of regular users. a) Read Only and b) Read Write.

·         Read Only users can access any information in the system, however they can not modify the content. This license costs $50 a month.

·         Read Write users can access AND MODIFY any data in the system. This license costs $85 a month per user.

·         A user can choose to cancel his account, in which case, the account status is set to ‘Expired’.

 

 

 

 

Step 4: You can also use this page to manage your Credit Card info.

·         For security reasons, we display ONLY the last 2 digits of your Credit Card number.

·         If you would like to use a different card , you can use this page to modify the details.

·         Please note that

o   On the first month, the billing is prorated. i.e. you sign up for a ReadOnly account on the 15th of the month, we will bill you 50% of your monthly bill.

o   After that, we bill at the highest license you were utilizing in that month. i.e if you were on a ReadWrite license for 10 days, and Read Only for 20 days, we will bill you for the Read Write license for the whole month.

Step 5: If your account status is expired, you are likely to see the whole page grayed out.

 

Step 4: If you are already inside a project, you can always get to this dashboard by clicking on ‘Return to Dashboard’ link shown below.

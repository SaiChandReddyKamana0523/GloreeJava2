Connect requirements

 

 

 

Login to your TraceCloud account.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Open a project.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 Open the requirement you want to connect to another requirement.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

On the Requirement Details page, click on the “Trace Actions” button and hit “Create Traces.” Suggestions come up as information is entered.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Individual traces can be seen below the “Trace Actions” button.

 

 

 

 

 

 All traces can be seen by clicking “Create a Trace Tree” within the “Folder Actions” dropdown.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 Enter the report’s requirements and click “Run Report” to view the trace tree.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 You can modify your report’s requirements by clicking “Modify Report.”

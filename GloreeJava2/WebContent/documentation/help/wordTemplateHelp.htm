Title: Word Template

 

Synopsis:  A reporting system that lets you embed your data base requirements formatted to suit your reporting needs in a Microsoft Word format.

 

Use Case:  A Cisco Product Manager has his Product Requirements in Trace Cloud. Cisco follows GEM (Great Engineering Methodology) that dictates that he needs to generate a word document in a certain format, with required collateral and store it in an internal Document Control System.

 

How it works:

 

Step1: Create your regular word document.  Put all the collateral you need in this document. I.e. images, graphs, sections or anything you would put in a normal document.

 

Step 2: At any place you want to embed Requirement Content, create a hyper link.  In Word 07, you can see the screen shot below. 

 

Step 3: Follow this syntax for getting requirements.

·         To embed a single requirement use PR-1 syntax.

·         To embed multiple requirements, Comma separates them. i.e. PR-1,PR-2,PR-3

·         To embed a range of requirements do this: PR-1...PR35

·         To embed all the requirements that would be returned by one of your saved reports (List or Trace Tree), do this : REPORTID-2026 ( where 2026 is the report Id)

·         Feel free to mix and match any of the above combinations.

 

See the screenshot below that summarizes them.

 

 

Step 4: Log in to your project and go to the folder where you want to store your template

 

 

 

Step 5: Upload this template.  You will notice it appear on the right hand side in ‘Folder Contents’ pane.

 

At this point, ANY user with read access to this project can open this template and either downloads the template to see its structure or Generate Report.

Step 6: Click on ‘Generate Report’.

At this point, you can select which attributes you want embedded for each requirement and what word format you would like your report to be.

 

 

Step 7: Save the generated report.
